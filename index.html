TURTLE CATEGORIES

Sri Lanka, nestled in the azure waters of the Indian Ocean, is home to a diverse and enchanting turtle population. Within the diverse turtle population of Sri Lanka, you'll encounter a fascinating array of species, each with its own unique characteristics and significance in the fragile marine ecosystems.

The coastal regions of Sri Lanka provide sanctuary to five out of the seven existing sea turtle species Throughout the year, these magnificent creatures embark on incredible journeys, returning to the same beaches where they were hatched to lay their own eggs. TURTLE HATCHERIES

Embark on a journey to discover the remarkable turtle hatcheries scattered across the stunning coastline of Sri Lanka. These hatcheries serve as sanctuaries for endangered turtle species, offering them a safe haven to nest and thrive.

Plan your visit to one or more of these turtle hatcheries and witness the extraordinary work being done to safeguard the future of these ancient sea turtles. Experience the thrill of releasing hatchlings into the ocean.

THREATS TO TURTLES

Discover the challenges that jeopardize turtle populations worldwide. Habitat loss, fisheries bycatch, and pollution pose severe risks to their survival. Explore the impacts of these threats and learn how we can prevent them, safeguarding the future of these remarkable creatures.

Turtles, with their ancient lineage and graceful presence, face numerous challenges that threaten their very existence. As human activities continue to impact our planet, it is crucial to understand and address these threats in order to protect and preserve these remarkable creatures.
