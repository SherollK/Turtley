HIKKADUWA HATCHERY

Where Conservation Meets Coastal Wonders

ABOUT

A remarkable destination nestled along Sri Lanka's stunning coastline. This renowned hatchery stands as a beacon of hope for endangered turtle species, offering a sanctuary where conservation efforts flourish.

One highlight is witnessing turtle nesting firsthand. See mother turtles coming ashore to lay their eggs, carefully selecting the perfect spot in the sand. Learn about the nesting process, the importance of protecting nests, and measures taken for their safe incubation.

Explore the hatchery and observe rescued turtles in tanks, providing an up-close encounter. Learn about different species, their characteristics, and conservation challenges. Engage with knowledgeable staff who foster a deeper appreciation for these marine beings.

Participate actively in conservation efforts by releasing hatchlings into the ocean. Witness their tiny flippers propelling them towards their home, symbolizing hope for the future of endangered species.
